Thunderbird Information (Mac)

General Information

Thunderbird, a free email application, allows you to receive, send, and read your electronic mail on your computer.

Minimum System Requirements

  • A Macintosh running macOS 10.9* or later
  • An Intel x86 processor
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 200 MB hard disk space

*Limited support for macOS 10.9 and 10.10
